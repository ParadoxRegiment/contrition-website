The Origin Point

This is the beginning of it all. A mysterious entity killed everything. Heroism died, crushed into
nothingness and scattered to the endless void, or so thought. The cores of many heroes were
fractured, broken, and dispersed into randomness. Some are suppressed memories, others are
too painful to remember. However, one thing's for sure. Things have changed, and they'll have to
find a reason why.

The Glutton?

The arrival of many falls into a city and nation of the unknown, where peace seems to be draped
around, or so it is thought. From where they fell, some have been implanted already, for an
unknown amount of time. Most fall on the outskirts, starting anew or trying to gain their
bearings. This is the beginning of our tale.

Story Event: The Crimson Wolves

Location: The Outer Ring

These individuals are caught up in the shrouding mystery, gathered in the city of Yù Huáng
Huán Yǔ Chéng. However, a group called the Crimson Wolves has been watching the outer ring
with mysterious, but malicious intentions. Some have encountered them directly in the walls,
and others are current bystanders. However, one thing's for certain — they're going to kick
them out of the Emperor's City. However, what will they learn from this?
